Enterprise Analytics Dashboard

Reducing cognitive load for financial analysts through progressive disclosure and data visualization.

Role Lead Product Designer
Timeline 8 Weeks
Impact +40% Efficiency

The Problem

Financial analysts were spending 20+ minutes generating reports due to a cluttered legacy interface. The data density was too high, leading to decision fatigue.

The Process

I conducted user interviews with 12 analysts and mapped their workflows. We found that 80% of the screen space was used by metrics they only checked once a month.

  • Discovery: Stakeholder interviews & Heatmaps.
  • Ideation: Sketching low-fi wireframes.
  • Design: Creating a high-fidelity design system.

The Solution

We implemented a "Dashboard Widget" system allowing users to pin their most critical metrics. Secondary data was moved to a drill-down view, cleaner the interface significantly.

40% Faster Reports
15% DAU Increase
