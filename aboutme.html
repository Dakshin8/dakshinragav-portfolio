Personal Background

Hi, I'm Dakshin Ragav, a User Experience Designer based in Chennai.

With nearly seven years of experience in UX design, I've had the opportunity to design digital products across different industries, creating experiences that solve real problems and deliver meaningful impact for users. My journey began in 2019 at my college senior's design agency, where working on live client projects gave me early exposure to the complete product design process and taught me the value of designing with purpose.

Over the years, I've been involved in every stage of the design journey, from understanding user needs and defining product strategy to creating wireframes, interaction designs, high-fidelity interfaces, prototypes, usability testing, and collaborating closely with developers to bring ideas to life. I enjoy taking ownership of the entire design process and believe the best products are built through curiosity, collaboration, and continuous iteration.

To strengthen my design foundation, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Visual Communication from Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology. Driven by my curiosity to better understand how people interact with digital products, I also completed a UX Design Certification at UXMint, Chennai, where I further developed my skills in user-centered design, research methodologies, design thinking, and product strategy.

The most rewarding part of my work is seeing the products I've designed being used by thousands of people. Knowing that my work helps make someone's experience simpler, faster, or more enjoyable is what motivates me to keep learning and improving with every project.

Looking ahead, my goal is to become a Design Leader, building products that create meaningful impact while mentoring the next generation of designers and helping them grow into thoughtful problem solvers.
